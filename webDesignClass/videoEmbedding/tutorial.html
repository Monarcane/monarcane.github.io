Step 1


Go to your selected service and find the "Share" button.


Step 2


In the share menu, find the button titled; "Embed".


Step 3


A menu similar to this one should open up, with a preview of the video and the needed "iframe" code.


Step 4


Select the entirety of the right "iframe" code. Press (Ctrl + C), or (Command + C) to copy the code.


Step 5


Find the spot in your code where you would like to embed the video. Press (Ctrl + V), or (Command + V) to paste the copied code into your webpage and save.


Finish


Voila, you now have a video embedded into your webpage.
